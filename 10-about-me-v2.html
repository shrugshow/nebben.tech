About Ben

Hey there!

My name is Ben, and I'm a 26 year old aspiring hand-model. Though not many people pursue this esteemed profession, I have been enamored with the industry ever since I saw my very first nail polish magazine ad. Who knew a mere hand could employ such poise and grace? Ever since then, my dream has been to dress like a businessman from the elbow down and have someone take picutes of me wearing a rolex. My father would always joke and say "This isn't what I imagined when my son said he wanted to become a Handy Man".

Anyways, I enjoy long walks on the beach, Barry Manilow, and rainy days. My hobbies include:

  • high fives
  • waving
  • clapping
  • handball

I've finally decided on putting myself out there and looking for that special someone! Hopefully this show can lend me a helping hand! Not to overplay my hand, but I think I'd make a spectacular contestant on this season of "The Bachelorette". Though sometimes I realize I can be a handful, I also know that I'm hands-down the right man for the show. With any luck, by this time next year I'll be taking the next Bachelorette's hand in marraige!
